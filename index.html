Exterior Surfaces

Concrete, Asphalt and Wood

  Moisture in the air and on the ground can cause moss and algae to grow on concrete and other surfaces.  We can pressure wash your driveways, sidewalks and decks to remove dirt and debris.  Then follow up with a chemical post treatment to inhibit re-growth.

Roof Cleaning

Asphalt, Terracotta and Metal 

  A roof may develop different types of algae, lichens, and fungi as bacteria take over.  When it dies it leaves dark streaks on your roof.  We soft wash your roof with a custom mixed chemical solution to kill the bacteria without damaging your shingles.

Exterior Facade

Brick, Stucco, Vinyl and Wood 

  Dirt, debris and tree sap can accumulate on your exterior walls over time. This layer provides mold and algae with a source of nutrients.  Depending on the material of the walls we offer both pressure washing, soft washing solutions and chemical applications to clean without damaging.
